Research Interests

I studied Physics at the University of Oxford from 2011-2015 gaining an MPhys. I then did a PhD at the University of Cambridge from 2015-2020, spending a year with the Wellcome Trust Mathematical Genomics and Medicine program before completing my thesis work with James Brenton at the CRUK-CI. I started my postdoctoral studies at the Cleveland Clinic Lerner Institute in early 2021 and am currently studying the topology of evolutionary processes in the lab of Jacob Scott whilst looking for further opportunities and faculty positions.
